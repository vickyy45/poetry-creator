About Us

Welcome to Webbasd Post Generator! Webbasd is a free and easy-to-use online tool that allows you to create beautiful Urdu poetry posts with custom backgrounds, stylish fonts, and creative layouts.

🎯 Our Vision

We believe that Urdu is not just a language but an emotion. Our vision is to bring poetry closer to the digital world by providing tools that combine the classical beauty of Urdu Nastaliq script with modern design trends.

  • Create customized poetry cards in just a few clicks.
  • Choose from different background gradients and colors.
  • Use elegant Urdu fonts for authentic expression.
  • Download and share your creations instantly on social media.
